Road Safety Omni Foundation Guidelines

  1. That I am joining and subscribing Road Safety Omni Foundation (RSOF) with my own consent without any force or pressure from any existing member of RSOF or from any source related to Organisation.
  2. That I shall render my services to RSOF free of cost on voluntarily basis, without claiming any kind of payments or service charges or expenses.
  3. That I shall not misuse the name of RSOF, strictly, if found liable to face action by Central Discipline Action Committee, which is Final.
  4. That I shall obey and respect the code of conduct and constitution/ Bye Laws of RSOF in all mode and all time.
  5. That I will work for Road Safety at least 4-6 hours in a week, and try to invest more time for Road Safety.
  6. That I shall authorize RSOF to put My Name, Photo & Detail on the RSOF website or literatures publish by RSOF.
  7. That I shall serve the RSOF with whatever responsibility and accountability given to me free of cost on voluntarily basis, without claiming any kind of payments or service charges or expenses.
  8. That I understand, My Membership is non-transferable, honorary, conditional & provisional and once the payment is made, it is non-refundable.
  9. That I shall not be involved with any kind of misbehavior or misconduct or anti India, anti government, illegal, misrepresentation, misleading, impersonation or anti society activity, which may result in Rejection / Termination of My Membership /Position of RSOF immediately and will be treated as criminal activity, where law is free to take any action. RSOF will not be held responsible for that.
  10. That I shall renew my membership within 30 days immediately after expiry if I further desire Membership. In case of change of address, I will inform the same to the RSOF office immediately through E-mail /Post with Address Proof.
  11. That in case of loss of the identity card, I will inform the nearest Police Station, RSOF Volunteer, RSOF Office in writing along with the Membership number immediately.
  12. That I have no objection if RSOF will hold Police verification of my character/Address or any matter which the RSOF vigilance team seems fit.
  13. If I have any doubt, question or problem will contact RSOF office in Written/Email.
  14. That matters of disqualification shall be decided at the sole discretion of the Board of Trustees.
  15. That all dispute subject to Faridabad Jurisdiction only.
  16. That all disputes are subject to arbitration only.
  17. That I shall take pre-approval in writing from RSOF Head office, before doing any correspondence with any Government or Non Government offices/ Establishment in India or Abroad.(Central/ State/ Local or International).
  18. That I have not been convicted by any Court in India.
